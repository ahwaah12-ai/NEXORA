FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

How long does a typical project take?
+
Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple landing page takes 2–3 weeks, a full web application 2–4 months, and enterprise products 4–8 months. We always provide detailed timelines upfront.
What's your development process?
+
We follow an agile methodology with weekly sprints and client check-ins. You'll always know what we're working on, with access to a live staging environment throughout development.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
+
Absolutely. We love working with startups and often offer equity-for-services arrangements for exceptional ideas. Book a free call and let's discuss what's right for your stage.
What happens after launch?
+
All plans include a post-launch support period. We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers, feature development, and growth partnerships. We're invested in your long-term success.
Do you sign NDAs?
+
Yes, always. We take confidentiality extremely seriously and are happy to sign your NDA before any discussions about your project begin.
Can you work with our existing codebase?
+
Yes. Our senior engineers are experienced at onboarding onto existing systems, refactoring legacy code, and adding new features without breaking what already works.

Still not sure?

Book a free 30-minute strategy session with our team. No sales pitch — just honest advice for your specific situation.
